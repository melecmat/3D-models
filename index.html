Matouš Melecký

Hello there! I am a Master's student of Informatics at TUM School of Computation, Information and Technology in Munich. My focus is in machine intelligence, computer vision and data analysis.

I am passionate about technology that enables human progress and positive change in people's lives all around the globe. I believe that computer vision has many exciting applications and research problems that need to be solved, which is why I chose it to be the centre of my professional focus. I am currently emplyed as a working student in Magazino GmbH in the Perception and Grasping team, where I am part of the development of computer vision pipelines that enable the robots Toru and Soto to work autonomously and reliably in real-world conditions at customer sites.
